Find your favorite food trucks

Pressed for time? Want to discover a new place to eat? Paycrave is a new app that allows you to find Food Trucks near your location, view menus, and order without having to wait in line.

Features

Search

Know where you want to eat? You can search for the name of the food truck. If you are somewhere new, you can use the location finder to find trucks near you.

View Menu

You can look at menus of each truck and decide what you’d like to eat. Once you have made your choices, you can just add the items to your cart!

Order & Pay

Once you are ready to checkout, just go to your cart and enter your credit card or Paypal information to pay through the app. If you’d like to pay, when you pick up, that is an option as well.

Pick Up

When you have placed your order, you will receive a confirmation and a time to pick up your order. All you have to do is walk up to the food truck, pick up your food and enjoy!
